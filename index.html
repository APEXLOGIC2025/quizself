Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use QuizSelf:

  • Host accounts: Name, email address, mobile number, city, country, organisation
  • Participants: Name and optional email address when joining a quiz
  • Quiz data: Questions, answers, scores, and time taken
  • Usage data: Device type, browser, and general location (country level)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the QuizSelf platform
  • To send quiz analytics reports to hosts (Paid and Premium plans)
  • To improve our services and fix technical issues
  • To display relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We use Firebase Authentication for secure login and Firebase Realtime Database for quiz data. Google's security standards apply to all stored data.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalised ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalised ads at google.com/settings/ads.

5. Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database
  • Google AdSense — advertisements
  • Netlify — website hosting

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Quiz results are visible only to the host who created the quiz and the admin. Participant names appear on the leaderboard of the quiz they attempted.

7. Data Deletion

Host accounts can be deleted by contacting us at [email protected]. Participant data is automatically removed when a quiz is deleted by the host.

8. Children's Privacy

QuizSelf may be used by students of all ages under the supervision of a teacher or institution. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at:
[email protected]

This privacy policy applies to the QuizSelf web application hosted at quiz-self.netlify.app and operated by Apex Logic.

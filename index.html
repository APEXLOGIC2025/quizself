Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Apexlogic / QuizSelf

QuizSelf ("we", "our", "us") is operated by Apexlogic (India). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use QuizSelf at quizself-five.vercel.app.

1. Information We Collect

Host accounts: Full name, email address, mobile number, city, country, and organisation/institution name collected during registration.

Quiz participants: Name and optional email address entered when joining a quiz. No account creation required.

Quiz activity data: Questions uploaded by hosts, participant answers, scores, time taken per question, and completion timestamps.

Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited — collected automatically via Firebase and Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the QuizSelf platform
  • Provide real-time leaderboards and score analytics to quiz hosts
  • Send email verification and account notifications
  • Display personalised advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Google Firebase Realtime Database servers hosted in the United States. Firebase enforces industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on QuizSelf. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads or by visiting aboutads.info.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us. Participant data (name, score) associated with a specific quiz may be retained by the quiz host for analytics purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

QuizSelf is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of QuizSelf after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Paid or Premium plan?

Plans are assigned by Apexlogic administrators. Write to us at apexlogic2025@gmail.com with your institution name and requirements and we will set up your plan within 1–2 business days.

Can I use QuizSelf for NEET or IIT-JEE mock tests?

Yes. QuizSelf supports negative marking, timed quizzes, and all question formats used in NEET and IIT-JEE. Many coaching institutes use QuizSelf specifically for competitive exam practice.

My quiz expired before all students could attempt it. What should I do?

Free plan quizzes are active for 1 day. If you need more time, contact us to upgrade your plan. Paid and Premium plans give 7 and 15 days of quiz activity respectively.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email your deletion request to apexlogic2025@gmail.com from your registered email address. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days as per our Privacy Policy.

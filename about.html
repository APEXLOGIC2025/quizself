Our Mission

At Apexlogic, we believe that every student in India deserves access to high-quality exam preparation tools — regardless of where they live or which institution they attend. We build technology that puts students and educators in control of how they learn and how they measure progress.

QuizSelf was born from a simple observation: most online quiz platforms are either too expensive for individual educators, too complex for students, or not designed for the specific demands of competitive exams like NEET and IIT-JEE. We built QuizSelf to solve all three problems at once.

What We Build

Apexlogic develops digital platforms focused on education and assessment. Our flagship product, QuizSelf, is a comprehensive quiz hosting and participation platform built specifically for the Indian education market.

QuizSelf Platform Features

QuizSelf supports multiple exam formats including NEET preparation, IIT-JEE mock tests, university internal examinations, and custom institutional quizzes. The platform features real-time leaderboards, flash quiz mode with speed-based scoring, negative marking, multi-answer question support, and detailed analytics for educators.

For Institutions

Educational institutions can enrol with Apexlogic to host their own branded quiz environment. Institution plans provide no-expiry quiz hosting, bulk student management, MIS reporting, and a dedicated super-admin dashboard for complete control over the assessment ecosystem.

For Students

Students can join any quiz using a simple quiz code — no registration required. After completing a quiz, students see their score breakdown, question-by-question analysis, and live leaderboard rankings compared to all other participants.
